What is Prop 66?

Proposition 66 would require that instead of the "Three-Strikes" law being put into action by any felony. The law would instead be prompted only by violent or serious felony. The "Three-Strikes" laws were passed with the intention to give harsher sentences to repeat offender or people with two prior felonies.
